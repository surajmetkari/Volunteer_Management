Green Horizons

Help with environmental conservation efforts in your area.

About Green Horizons

Green Horizons is dedicated to preserving and restoring natural habitats. Volunteers participate in activities like tree planting, habitat restoration, and community clean-ups to promote environmental health.

Volunteer Responsibilities

  • Assist with planting native trees and plants.
  • Help remove invasive species.
  • Participate in clean-up and conservation projects.
  • Engage with the community to raise environmental awareness.

Benefits

  • Gain hands-on experience in environmental conservation.
  • Connect with like-minded individuals who care about the planet.
  • Make a lasting positive impact on the community and environment.
